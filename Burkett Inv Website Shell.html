Who We Are

Burkett Investments, LLC is a Nashville-based real estate consulting and investment management firm founded by two seasoned professionals with deep roots in the industry.

We specialize in providing institutional-grade real estate consulting services to clients navigating complex markets, investment decisions, and portfolio strategy across the Southeast.

Our approach combines decades of hands-on operational experience with a client-first philosophy — delivering thoughtful, data-driven guidance at every stage of the investment lifecycle.

Our Leadership

The Faces Behind
Burkett Investments

Kyle Burkett

Co-Owner & Principal

With over 20 years of experience in the real estate industry, Kyle brings a wealth of expertise spanning multifamily, senior housing, self-storage, retail, and short-term rental investments. He holds an MBA from Alliance Manchester Business School and the CAPS designation, and has served as a senior leader at Nicol Investment Company, Greystar, and Pegasus Residential. A board member of The Gulch Nashville, Kyle is deeply embedded in the Nashville real estate community and brings institutional-level insight to every engagement.

Trey Burkett

Co-Owner & Principal

Trey is a Project Management Professional (PMP) and University of Illinois graduate with a background in finance, operations, and strategic project management. He brings a disciplined, analytical approach to real estate investing, combining rigorous process management with a sharp eye for market opportunity. Trey's operational expertise and financial acumen serve as a strong complement to the firm's advisory and investment management capabilities.
